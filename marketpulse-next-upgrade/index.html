KEEP IT REAL

What the signal engine actually means

Are these signals real?

Yes, in the sense that they are computed from real market data and explicit rules. No, in the sense that no signal can guarantee profit. This is a decision tool, not a magic ATM.

Can I use this to place real-money trades?

Not yet. This build is set up around Alpaca paper trading first, which is the safer move while you test the logic.

Will it scale for other users later?

Yep. Once your private version feels right, the next step is moving the keys behind user auth instead of your single-account setup.
